How to Use the QR Converter

1. Choose the type of QR code you want to generate (Text, Link, VCard, Email, or Wi-Fi).
2. Fill in the relevant fields. For example, if you selected “Link,” enter your URL.
3. Click the “Generate QR Code” button to instantly see your QR code.
4. Scan the code with your phone’s camera or any QR scanner to verify it.
